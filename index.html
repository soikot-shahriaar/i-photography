Wildlife Photography


Wildlife Photography can bring out some of the most intense photographers you’ll meet – people who might spend hours on end waiting for an animal to wander into the perfect spot or search for creatures that most people would rather try to avoid.

Astrophotography


Astrophotography involves taking photographs of the night sky or deep space objects. This can involve simple star trails, pictures of the moon, the milky way or other celestial objects or deep space images of objects such as nebulae or galaxies.

Fashion Photography


Fashion Photography is a genre of photography which is devoted to displaying fashion items or haute couture. Fashion photographers take pictures of a dressed model in a studio or an outside setting. Fashion photography is most often conducted for advertisements or fashion magazines

Sports Photography


Sports Photography is a type of photography that covers every sport and sporting event specially football, basketball and baseball. Its like capturing the action of a game and behind the scenes of the players. This type of photography helps to promote brands, the players, as well as the sport.
